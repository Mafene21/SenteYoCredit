About Sente Yo Credits

Established in 2005, Sente Yo Credits is Uganda's leading digital-first bank, committed to innovation and financial inclusion.

🏢 Company Information

Branches: 25+ across Uganda

Employees: 500+ dedicated professionals

🌟 Our Mission

To empower communities through accessible, secure, and modern banking solutions that drive economic growth and financial well-being.

We strive to make banking simple, affordable, and available to every Ugandan, regardless of location or income level.

👁️ Our Vision

To be the most trusted and innovative financial partner in East Africa, setting the standard for digital banking excellence.

By 2030, we aim to serve 1 million customers across East Africa with cutting-edge financial technology.
